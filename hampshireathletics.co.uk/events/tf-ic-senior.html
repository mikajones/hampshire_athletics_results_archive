8-Counties Senior Inter-Counties Match

This was an invitation match between the following counties: Essex, Hampshire, Hertfordshire, Kent, Middlesex, Oxfordshire, Surrey and Sussex. The participating counties hosted the fixture in rotation.

After disappointing attendances, particularly in 2019, and having been cancelled in 2020 and 2021, this match was replaced in 2022 by the SEAA Senior/Under 20 Inter-Counties Championships.
