Please read before entering the Championships

  1. First check that you are qualified to compete in the Hampshire Championships:
    • You must have been born in Hampshire, the Isle of Wight or the Channel Islands, or
    • You must have been resident continuously or in H.M. Forces service in these areas for at least nine months prior to the championships.

      and
    • You must not have competed for another county or in another county's championships during the preceding year.
    The QuadKids Championships are intended for but not restricted to Hampshire athletes.
    Non-qualifying athletes wishing to compete in the women's steeplechase, also athletes wishing to compete as guests in the Multi-Events Championships, and wishing to enter online should .
  2. Payment will be taken by PayPal, using your PayPal account if you have one, or using your debit or credit card if you prefer. To offset the charges that Hampshire Athletics has to pay for using the PayPal service, we'll ask you to pay an extra 50p per athlete, regardless of the number of events entered.
  3. Please use the buttons at the foot of each page for navigating through the entry process rather than the forward and backward arrows on your internet browser. The online forms have been tested using the most common browsers (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari and Opera).

1.  Athlete's details

Fill in as directed, then click the Next button.

2.  Events entered

Tick the box next to each event that you wish to enter, and enter your previous best performance if you have one, then click the Next button.

3.  Check entry details

Check that all the details entered on the previous two pages are correct, then click the Next button.

Your PayPal Shopping cart

A summary of your entry will be placed in your shopping cart. At this point you can Continue Shopping to enter another athlete, or Check Out to pay for your entry or entries.

Follow the PayPal instructions. You can Cancel and return to Hampshire Athletics at any point until you pay.

After payment, please Click to complete the entry process and record your payment in our database.

Important: PayPal does not release any sensitive information to Hampshire Athletics about your payment details. We do collect your transaction and receipt id's to check that you've paid for your entry.

4.  Your entry has been accepted

You can print your entry details for future reference. Within a few minutes, you ought to receive an email from PayPal confirming your payment and an email from Hampshire Athletics accepting your entry. We will not refund any entry fees for online (or postal) entries after they have been accepted.

Later, normally on the same day or the following day, we will verify your entry and payment, and send you a confirmation email.
