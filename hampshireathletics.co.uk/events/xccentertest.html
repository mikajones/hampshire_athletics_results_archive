This year you can enter the Saucony Hampshire Cross-Country Championships online.

Please read before entering the Championships

  1. All competitors must be qualified to compete in the Hampshire Championships:
    • You must have been born in Hampshire, the Isle of Wight or the Channel Islands, or
    • You must have been resident continuously or in H.M. Forces service in these areas for at least nine months prior to the championships.

      and
    • You must not have competed for another county or in another county's championships during the preceding year.
  2. Payment will be taken by PayPal, using your PayPal account if you have one, or using your debit or credit card if you prefer. There is no extra charge for using this service.
  3. Please use the buttons at the foot of each page for navigating through the entry process rather than the forward and backward arrows on your internet browser. The online forms have been tested using the most common browsers (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari and Opera).

If you are a Team Manager wanting to enter athletes on behalf of a club, go to the Team Manager instructions further down this page.

For athletes entering individually ...

1.  Athlete's details

2.  Check entry details

Check that all the details entered on the previous page are correct, then click the Next button.

Your PayPal Shopping cart

A summary of your entry will be placed in your shopping cart

If you want to enter another athlete without having to enter your payment details twice, click Continue Shopping.

Otherwise, click Check Out to pay for your entry or entries.

Follow the PayPal instructions. You can Cancel and return to Hampshire Athletics at any point until you pay.

After payment, please click View your entry details.

Important: PayPal does not release any sensitive information to Hampshire Athletics about your payment. We do collect your transaction and receipt id's to check that you've paid for your entry.

3.  Your entry has been accepted

You can print your entry details for future reference. Within a few minutes, you should receive an email from PayPal confirming your payment and an email from Hampshire Athletics accepting your entry. We will not refund any entry fees for online (or postal) entries after they have been accepted.

For Team Managers ...

1.  Complete a Manager application form

This allows us to check that you are a bona fide team manager for your club. There is no charge for this.

Fill in your details as directed, then click the Next button.

Check that all the details entered on the previous page are correct, then click the Next button.

An email will be sent to the Entry Secretary. You should receive a reply in the next couple of days. This will contain an entry number that you can use to manage your team entry.

Click the button to apply to be a Team Manager

2.  Enter your team

Use the entry number that you have been given to access the Team Manager form, with your email address, then click the Next button.

Now Add entries, up to 20 at a time, until all your athletes have been recorded. Remember to click Save each time.

Continue to add your athletes' details. If necessary, you can click Finish and continue later; your data will be saved in the meantime. When you're happy your entry is complete, click Pay and follow the PayPal instructions. Within a few minutes, you should receive an email from PayPal confirming your payment and an email from Hampshire Athletics accepting your entries.

Please note that you can delete athletes from your entry list before payment, but once they have been paid for they cannot be changed.

After paying for your initial entry, you can still add and pay for further entries.

At any time you can click List all to see all your club's athletes who have entered, including those who have entered individually. This should avoid athletes being either entered twice or omitted accidentally.

After you've been authorised, click the button to manage your team
