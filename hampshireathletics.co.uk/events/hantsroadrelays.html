Lakeside Road Relays
incorporating the Hampshire Road Relay Championships

We are pleased to announce the Lakeside Road Relays, incorporating the Hampshire Road Relay Championships and Under 11's Race, to take place on Sunday 12 April 2015 at 1000 Lakeside, Cosham, Portsmouth.

  • Relays for Under 13, Under 15, Under 17, Seniors & Veterans
  • Plus 2k race for Under 11's
  • OPEN TO ALL UKA AFFILIATED CLUBS

Hopefully this will become an annual event if sufficient numbers of teams show interest.
