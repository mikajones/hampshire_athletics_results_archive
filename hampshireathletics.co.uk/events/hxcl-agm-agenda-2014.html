  7. New Membership Affiliation/Resignation
  8. Amendments to Constitution
    1. A third subscription tier to be introduced and applied to clubs fielding very few runners in the previous season. The clubs are to be selected by the committee.
      The committee envisages that this rate for the coming season will be £50 and will apply to no more than five clubs.
      Note: new members will always pay the mid-rate in their first season.
      Proposed by the committee.
    2. To include the U11 age group as part of the formal race programme and competition within the Hampshire Cross Country League.
      Proposed by Southampton Athletic Club.
