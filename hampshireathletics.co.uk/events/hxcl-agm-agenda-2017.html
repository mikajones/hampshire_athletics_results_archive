  8. Amendments to Constitution
    1. Proposed by the committee, to take effect for the forthcoming season:
      Currently - Rule 2: Five fixtures will be held each season, all five counting towards Team Results.
      Proposed - Rule 2: Five fixtures will be held each season, the best four performances counting towards Team Results at the end of the season.
      Currently - Rule 11: Team awards will be made to clubs competing in a total of five meetings each season.
      Proposed - Rule 11: Team awards will be made to clubs calculated on the best four performances out of five in the season.
    2. Proposed by the committee, to take effect for the forthcoming season:
      If Proposal 8.1 is adopted, in the event of a cancelled fixture, the best three team performances will decide team awards, i.e. as for individuals.
    3. Proposed by the committee, to take effect for the forthcoming season:
      Divisions 1 and 2 of the Male Veterans to be merged.
    4. Proposed by the committee, to take effect for the forthcoming season:
      In order to encourage smaller clubs, the committee shall have the power to reduce a member club's affiliation fee to the bottom tier.
