SPORTSHALL added 12 January 2014

Sportshall Fun in Athletics South-East Regional Festival 2014

The Hampshire Under 11s' teams repeated last year's performances in the Sportshall Fun in Athletics South-East Regional Festival, which took place in Burgess Hill on Sunday 12 January. The girls were placed fifth (out of nine teams) and the boys sixth (out of eight). Both competitions were won by Kent.

Full results will be published on the national Sportshall website in the next few days.

SPORTSHALL added 11 March 2013

Under 13 Girls qualify for Sportshall UK Finals

Hampshire's Under 13 Girls' team have qualified for the Sportshall UK Finals as closest runners-up. This means there will be three Hampshire teams at the LG Arena, Birmingham on Sunday 7 April, the Under 13 Girls, and the Under 15 Girls and Under 13 Boys who both won their Regional Finals last month.
