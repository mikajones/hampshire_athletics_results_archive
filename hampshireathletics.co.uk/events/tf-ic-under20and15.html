SEAA Under 20 and Under 15 Inter-Counties Championships

This competition was between all the 19 constituent counties in the South of England Athletic Association region: Bedfordshire, Berkshire, Buckinghamshire, Cambridgeshire, Cornwall, Devon, Dorset, Essex, Hampshire, Hertfordshire, Kent, Middlesex, Norfolk, Oxfordshire, Somerset, Suffolk, Surrey, Sussex and Wiltshire.

It was replaced in 2022 by the SEAA Senior/Under 20 Inter-Counties Championships and the SEAA Under 17/Under 15 Inter-Counties Championships.
