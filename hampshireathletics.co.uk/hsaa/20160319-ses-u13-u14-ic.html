South East Schools' Inter-Counties U13 & U14 Cross-Country Championships
Saturday 19 March at Popham Airfield, Basingstoke

Hampshire Schools' AA hosted the Inter-Counties Under 13 & Under 14 Cross-Country Championships at Popham Airfield, Basingstoke.

The counties taking part were Essex, Hampshire, Kent, Surrey and Sussex.
