SCHOOLS updated 24 January 2023

Hampshire Schools' AA Cross-Country Championships

Unfortunately the Championships had to be cancelled. Temperatures overnight were significantly lower than forecast the day before! After inspections between 08:15 and 09:15, consideration of delayed start times and looking at adapted courses, a group of HSAA officers decided that the only safe action was to cancel, bearing in mind that jogging around sections of the park was very different to athletes racing in dangerous conditions.

Even by midday, large sections of the course remained icy and rutted, and linking sections of the Sports Centre for any course was impossible.

We offer our sincere apologies to athletes, parents and teachers, many of whom still attended on Saturday morning.

English Schools Cross-Country Championships

For the above age groups held in March. Teams of 12 are selected in each age group and are entered. Teams are selected from results in the Hampshire Schools Championships and the Inter-County match. A final 8 are then selected to compete. This will usually involve an overnight stay depending on the venue. Travel will be by team coach and all athletes must travel and stay with the team. The first 8 Junior and Intermediate athletes at this event are selected to compete in the Schools (SIAB) Home Countries International Match.
