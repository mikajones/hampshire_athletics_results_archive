English Schools Cross-Country Championships

For the above age groups held in March. Teams of 12 are selected in each age group and are entered. Teams are selected from results in the Hampshire Schools Championships and the Inter-County match. A final 8 are then selected to compete. This will usually involve an overnight stay depending on the venue. Travel will be by team coach and all athletes must travel and stay with the team. The first 8 Junior and Intermediate athletes at this event are selected to compete in the Schools (SIAB) Home Countries International Match.
