ESAA National Cross-Country Championships 2020
14 March at Sefton Park, Liverpool

Hampshire Schools' teams had another successful day at the New Balance ESAA Cross Country Championships.

The Junior Boys and Girls achieved the best results of the Hampshire teams. Aldershot, Farnham & District AC colleagues, James Dargan and Dan Shattock finished in fourth and sixth places and led the Hampshire and Vectis Junior Boys' team to the silver-medal position. Robyn Fossa and her Southampton AC clubmate Annie Mann finished sixth and eighth in identical times, with the Junior Girls' team taking the bronze medals. Alice Garner in sixteenth place also led the Senior Girls' team to third position, and Henry McLuckie, fifteenth in the Senior Boys' race, headed the Senior Boys' team to seventh place. Both the Intermediate Boys' team and Intermediate Girls' team finished in eleventh position in their respective competitions. Hampshire and Vectis came second in the Girls' Aggregate Trophy and third in the Boys' Aggregate Trophy.

Other notable top-50 results were:

  • Senior Boys - Edward Wright (49th)
  • Intermediate Boys - Ben Brown (34th) and Campbell Laird (37th)
  • Junior Boys - Harry Prescott (39th), James Lewis (44th), George Lambert (50th) and Stanley Parkinson (51st)
  • Senior Girls - Abigail Woolaston (27th)
  • Intermediate Girls - Isabelle Hinkley (26th) and Rose Chesterfield (50th)
  • Junior Girls - Elizabeth Atkinson, representing Channel Islands Schools, finished in 28th place.
