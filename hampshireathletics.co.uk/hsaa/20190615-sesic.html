South Eastern Schools' Inter-Counties Championships
Saturday 15 June 2019 at Erith Stadium, Bexley, Kent

Results

This year teams were competing for the Cunliffe Trophy, named in memory of Geoff Cunliffe, Hampshire's former Secretary for several years, and member of the ESAA General Committee.

Results are now available, with thanks to Kent Schools' AA.

Location

Erith Leisure Centre is situated in Avenue Road, Erith. It is near the A206 which meets the M25 just south of the Dartford Crossing. The postcode is DA8 3AT.
