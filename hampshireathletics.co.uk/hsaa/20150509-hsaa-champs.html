Hampshire Schools' Championships

Multi Events - Thursday 21st May at Andover at 1.00pm

This event has been moved to Charlton Sports Centre, Andover owing to track repairs at Down Grange, Basingstoke.

Entry forms for this event will be in Schools this week. If you are keen to enter please see your PE Teacher as they have to make the entry. If you experience problems please contact Eileen Williams and I will send you the entry form which you can take into School.

We would expect you to be able to achieve the basic standard in all 5 events and the 'A' standard in at least 2 events.

Track and Field Championships - Saturday 13th June at Portsmouth

Senior Events:

There will be no Senior 200m or 800m events. No Girls 100H or 400H. No Boys Steeplechase or 3000m.

However, if you are desperate for the opportunity to do these events at this championship please contact Eileen Williams. Also please contact Eileen if you have an entry standard and wish to compete at the SEAA Championships on Sat 13th June.

English Schools Championships - 10th and 11th July at Gateshead

Selection for this event will again be very difficult as we have well over 40 athletes with an entry standard at the moment and we are only permitted to take a total of 60 athletes, so an entry standard is not a guarantee of selection for this prestigious event.

Once again those with an entry standard or are very close will be asked to declare their details online at www.esaa.net

This facility is already available so please go to Athlete Registration and follow the instructions.
