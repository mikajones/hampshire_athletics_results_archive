South East Schools U13/U14 Inter-Counties Cross-Country Championships 2019
Saturday 23 March
Christ's Hospital, Sussex

Sussex Schools AA hosted the South East Inter-Counties Schools Cross-Country Championships, for Under 13 and Under 14 age groups at Christ's Hospital, near Horsham, West Sussex.

In a match where the honours were spread fairly evenly among the counties taking part, Hampshire's individual athletes provided two champions and one runner-up, while the teams achieved one first, one second and one third place in the four age groups.

The Year 7 Boys' race was Hampshire's most successful, as James Lewis and Oliver Stephenson came first and second respectively and, not surprisingly, the team also finished first. Evie Bailey in eighth place led the Year 7 Girls' team home to fifth position.

Robyn Fossa was Hampshire's other individual Champion in the Year 8 Girls' race, where the team came third. Dan Shattock and James Dargan finished fourth and fifth in the Year 8 Boys' race, leading the team to a runners'-up spot.
