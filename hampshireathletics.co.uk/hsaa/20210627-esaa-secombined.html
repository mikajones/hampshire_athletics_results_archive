English Schools AA
South East Combined Events Championships 2021
26/27 June at Lee Valley Athletics Centre

The Hampshire Schools combined events team had a very successful weekend at the South East regional finals in Lee Valley.

Oliver Thatcher and George Asprey took 1st and 2nd place respectively in the Senior Boys' Decathlon, Oliver recording an impressive point total that takes him into the world's top 40 for his age group.

Senior Girl Yasmin Grosvenor unfortunately was DQ'd in her first event, the hurdles, in her heptathlon and bravely completed the first day but understandably did not return for the second. She has since been invited to the National Finals in view of her previous performances.

Grace Colmer won the Intermediate Girls' event in her first ever competitive heptathlon. There were no competitors in the Inter Boys Octathlon.

The Junior Boys' team of Archie Ellis, Nicholas Thomas, George Musselwhite and Ruben Price performed well, coming 4th as a team with Archie winning a place through to the national finals. The Junior Girls' team of Abi Belward, Rebekah Smith, Carys Jeffries and Ella Hutton were 3rd as a team with Abi and Rebekah invited to take part in the national finals in September.

Andy Fisher
Combined Events Team Manager
