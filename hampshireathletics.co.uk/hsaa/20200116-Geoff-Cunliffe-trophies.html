Geoff Cunliffe trophies

Geoff Cunliffe

In memory of Geoff, who served as Hampshire Schools' AA Secretary for almost 30 years, Hampshire Schools wish to provide two trophies.

These will be awarded annually to the athlete of the year and also Team of the year. Nominations will come from District Associations with the final decisions being made by Officers of the Association.

HSAA would welcome donations, large or small, to help fund these Awards and a collection box will be in Registrations at the Cross Country Meetings, commencing this Saturday 18th, and also at the Track & Field Meetings.

For any further information please contact Eileen Williams.
