South Eastern Schools' Inter-Counties match
Saturday 18 June 2016 at the David Weir Leisure Centre, Carshalton, Surrey

Hampshire Schools came third overall at the South Eastern Schools' Inter-Counties Championships, which took place at the David Weir Leisure Centre, Carshalton, just two points behind hosts Surrey.

Hampshire came in joint first place in the Intermediate Girls' match, and came second in the Junior Girls' and Junior Boys' matches. Full results are now available, with thanks to Surrey Schools AA:
