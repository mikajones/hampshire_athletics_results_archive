Hampshire Schools' Athletic Association

Hampshire Schools' Athletic Association organise County competitions for all Secondary Schools in Hampshire and the Isle of Wight, and select and accompany teams to Inter County fixtures and National Championships. All County teams are accompanied by accredited Team managers.
