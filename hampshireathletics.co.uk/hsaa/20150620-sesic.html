South Eastern Schools' Inter-Counties match
Saturday 20 June 2015 at Erith Stadium, Bexley

Hampshire Schools had a successful day at the South Eastern Schools' Inter-Counties Championships, which took place at Erith Stadium, Avenue Road, Bexley, DA8 3AT on Saturday 20 June.

Hampshire won the Intermediate Boys' match outright, and came joint first with the hosts Kent in an evenly contested Junior Boys' match. Second place in the Inter Girls' match and fourth in the Junior Girls' event resulted in second place behind Kent in the overall competition.
