South Eastern Schools' Inter-County Match
Saturday 21 June at K2, Crawley

Though Surrey won three of the age groups to Kent's one, Kent were the overall winners of this match.

Hampshire's best result was a second place in the Inter Girls match, though their third-place finishes in the Inter and Junior Boys' matches were only two or three points behind the second-placed teams.
