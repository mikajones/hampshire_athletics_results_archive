Hampshire team for ESAA Track & Field Championships
Friday 11 and Saturday 12 July 2014

The Hampshire team selection for the 2014 English Schools' AA Track & Field Championships has been announced.

Although 94 athletes had achieved the entry qualification standard, the county is limited to 60 entries. So as well as congratulating those who have been selected, we should commiserate with those who have had to be left out of the team.
