South Eastern Schools' Inter-Counties match
Saturday 17 June 2017 at Down Grange, Basingstoke

An extremely close match for much of a hot and sunny day, with Kent and Surrey eventually sharing first place on 560 points, hosts Hampshire third on 530 points and Sussex on 450 points.
