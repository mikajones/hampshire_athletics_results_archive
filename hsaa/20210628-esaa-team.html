Hampshire team for ESAA Track & Field Championships
Friday 9, Saturday 10 and Sunday 11 July 2021

A total of 54 athletes have been selected to represent Hampshire at the 2021 English Schools' AA Track & Field Championships at the Regional Athletics Centre, Manchester on Friday 9 July (Senior Girls and Boys), Saturday 10 July (Intermediate Girls and Boys) and Sunday 11 July (Junior Girls and Boys).

Having first been nominated by Hampshire, a maximum of 16 athletes across all counties were selected by ESAA to compete in each event. Congratulations to these athletes on their selection!
