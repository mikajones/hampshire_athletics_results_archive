ESAA National Cross-Country Championships 2022
19 March at Hop Farm, Paddock Wood, Kent

At the ESAA Cross-Country Championships, Hampshire had a successful day winning gold, silver and bronze medals, and being the third-best boys' team overall.

Team results and top-30 finishers were:

  • Junior Girls (2nd) - Matilda Bengtsson (14th) and Penny Worthy (19th)
  • Junior Boys (1st) - Archie Pearson (3rd) and Ruben Price (9th)
  • Inter Girls (26th)
  • Inter Boys (3rd) - James Dargan (2nd) and Harry Wilson (26th)
  • Senior Girls (17th) - Lily Neate (28th)
  • Senior Boys (6th) - Ben Brown (9th) and Woody Jerome (19th)
