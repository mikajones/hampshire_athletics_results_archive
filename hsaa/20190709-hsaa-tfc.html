Trophy Presentations

16:30 PRESENTATIONS of both TEAM & INDIVIDUAL TROPHIES - these should finish at about 16:45. Districts are encouraged to stay until they are concluded.

TEAM TROPHIES - these will be awarded to the winning: Year 7 Girls team, Year 7 Boys team, Year 8 Girls team and Year 8 Boys team.
Also, two Overall Points Aggregate Team Trophies will be presented to either Aldershot or Eastleigh & Winchester in the 1st Division, and another to the winner from the other 8 Districts in the 2nd Division.

INDIVIDUAL TROPHIES - these will be presented for the Best Track Performance and Best Field Performance of the day, across all four competitions.
