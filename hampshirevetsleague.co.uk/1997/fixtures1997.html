Fixtures and Results 1997

For this year only, there was a small change in the scoring for the divisional matches. The same numbers of points were awarded in all events, i.e. 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2 and 1 points were given in all of the A-, B- and Over 50 strings.
