Fixtures and Results 2011

This year the Hants & Surrey Division adopted the same scoring system as had been used by the other divisions and in the final for several years. The same numbers of points, i.e. 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2 and 1, were awarded in every event. As each league now had eight teams for the first time since 1998, this applied to both men and women.
