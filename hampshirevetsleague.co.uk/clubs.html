Clubs

The following clubs are currently competing in the Hampshire Vets League:

  • Aldershot, Farnham & District AC
  • Basingstoke & Mid Hants AC
  • City of Portsmouth AC
  • Fleet & Crookham AC
  • Havant AC
  • Overton Harriers & AC
  • Southampton AC
  • Winchester & District AC

Other clubs that have competed since 1995 include:

  • Camberley & District AC (2006-2008)
  • Chichester Runners & AC (until 2001)
  • Guildford & Godalming AC (until 2005)
  • Haslemere Border AC (2001-2008)
  • Portsmouth Royal Navy AC (2006)
  • Stubbington Green Runners & AC (2007)
  • Woking AC (until 2012)
