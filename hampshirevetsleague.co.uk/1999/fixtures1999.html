Fixtures and Results 1999

Age-grading was abandoned after one season, probably owing to the problems compiling the results without a computer to hand. Instead, for 1999 and all years since, M60 and W60 strings were added to one track event and one field event in each divisional match.

About half of the events in the four final matches this year also included over-60 strings. Women's Final One and Men's Final Three were between the respective divisional champions while Women's Final Two and Men's Final Four were between the runners-up.

Of the Hants & Surrey qualifiers, Havant's women and men both achieved narrow wins in Finals One and Four and City of Portsmouth competed in Men's Final Three. However Woking did not appear in the women's runners-up final.
