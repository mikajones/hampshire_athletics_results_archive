Fixtures and Results 2000

This year, there were no changes in the divisional matches but a small change in the final matches. Women's Final One and Men's Final Three were again between the divisional champion clubs but Women's Final Two and Men's Final Four were now between "composite" teams representing the other clubs in each division.

Hants & Surrey didn't appear in either of the "composite" finals, and the five men's and three women's teams that did compete consisted largely or exclusively of members of the club that had finished their division as runners-up.
