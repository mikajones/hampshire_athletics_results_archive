8-Counties Under 17 Inter-Counties Match

This was an invitation match between the following counties: Buckinghamshire, Essex, Hampshire, Hertfordshire, Kent, Middlesex, Surrey and Sussex. The participating counties hosted the fixture in rotation.

This match was cancelled in 2020 and 2021, and it was replaced in 2022 by the SEAA Under 17/Under 15 Inter-Counties Championships.
