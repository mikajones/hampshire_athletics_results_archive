Parking

  • The car park is to be found by following the road with the grandstand on your left and just as you go over the brow of the hill there is a large car park adjacent to the road on the right.
  • Please use the car park - it is large enough for everybody. Please do not park on the road.
  • The start and finish area is right next to the car park.
