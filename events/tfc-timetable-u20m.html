Competitors' notes

Please report to Competitors' Registration in the Track Room at least one hour before your first event each day so that we know that you will be competing and track heats can be seeded fairly.

Please tell us the numbers of your events to make registration quicker.

If you are competing on both Saturday and Sunday, keep your numbers safe as you need to wear the same numbers on each day.

Track finals will be run at the time scheduled for the heats if insufficient competitors register on the day to require heats.
