Under 17 Men & Women Inter-County Match
Mountbatten Centre, Portsmouth
Sunday 20 July 2014

Full results of the Under 17 Inter-County match held at the Mountbatten Centre, Portsmouth on Sunday are now available.

Congratulations to Kent who won both the Men's and Women's match. Although they made their intentions clear early on, there were enough battles going for placings throughout the field to keep everyone interested.

We hope that all athletes, officials, coaches and spectators had a successful and enjoyable day.
