Hampshire Athletics Results

This website contains an archive copy of the results previously held on the Hampshire Athletics and Hampshire Vets League websites.

Richard Dean was the dedicated force behind the old Hampshire Athletics and Vets League websites, ensuring every event and result was meticulously recorded and accessible. His passion and commitment left a lasting impact on the community. The new archive site honors his legacy, preserving the history he so carefully documented for future generations.
