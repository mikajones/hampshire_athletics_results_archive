English Schools' Athletic Association
Track & Field Championships 2015
10-11 July at Gateshead

Highlights of a very successful couple of days:

  • 5 Gold medals
  • 4 Silver medals
  • 4 Bronze medals
  • 6 Internationals: Mair Edwards; Holly Mills; Tom Miller; James Beeks; Adam Jones; Andrew Douglas
  • 7 x 4th places; 7 x 5th places; 5 x 6th places; 1 x 7th place; 3 x 8th places = Total 36 in top 8
  • 34 x NS; 25 ES were achieved including those in heats
  • 47 contributed to team points including those who ran in relays
  • 21 PB/SB were recorded
  • Weather mainly dry and sunny. Variable headwind in home straight
  • 106 standards prior to selection

Comparison with 2014:

  • Fewer medals but more golds: More Internationals: Similar standards and PB/SBs:
  • Fewer scoring points but better lower placings

Overall:

Girls5th103WinnerSurrey175
Boys3rd139WinnerHertfordshire161

Eileen Williams,
Hampshire Team Manager

In addition, competing for the Channel Islands, Stanley Livingston won a gold medal in the Senior Boys' 400m Hurdles, and bronze medals were won by Alastair Chalmers in the Inter Boys' 400m Hurdles and Edward Mason in the Inter Boys' 1500m Steeplechase.
