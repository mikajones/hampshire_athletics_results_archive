Leisure Pursuits Hampshire League 2002/3
30 November at Prospect Park, Reading (Match 3)

The damp, dull, misty conditions did not deter the numbers competing at Reading. Our thanks to Reading A.C. for preparing such a well marked course and hosting the fixture - the supply of hot dogs wafting the air has become something to be expected.

The Competition Some good individual runs from Basingstoke, Chichester and Stubbington Green but Aldershot once again proved their strength in depth and in addition to their individual flair. Once again in the Senior Men's League Team Solent returned a potential Division 1 result. The Senior Ladies team competition proved to be very close with just three points separating the first three places.
