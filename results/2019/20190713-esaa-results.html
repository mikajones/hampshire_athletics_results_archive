English Schools' Athletic Association
Track & Field Championships 2019
12-13 July at Birmingham

Highlights:

  • Weather dry, sun and clouds and a fierce wind on Friday
  • 7 Gold medals
  • 3 Silver medals
  • 7 Bronze medals
  • 6 x 4th places; 2 x 5th places; 8 x 6th places; 4 x 7th place; 3 x 8th places = Total 40 in top 8
  • There were 16 entry standards and 28 National standards (only 1 per athlete counted)
  • 56 athletes contributed towards team points (including those who ran in relays)
  • 2 athletes with medal potential withdrawn for international competition
  • 2 athletes with medal potential withdrawn through injury

Overall 1 more medal than last year but no internationals - first time for a number of years

Much better - number of standards (especially NS), people getting points, PBs, minor places (4th-8th)

Eileen Williams
